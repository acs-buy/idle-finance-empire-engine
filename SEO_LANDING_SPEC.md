# SEO_LANDING_SPEC.md

## Pages
1) / (Landing - indexable)
2) /play (Game)
3) /changelog (optional)
4) /privacy
5) /terms

## Landing Structure
- Hero:
  - H1: “Build your investing empire with compounding.”
  - Sub: “A finance idle game. No signup. Play in your browser.”
  - CTA: “Play Free” -> /play
- Social proof:
  - short bullets: “Offline earnings”, “Macro events”, “Prestige resets”
- How it works (3 steps)
- Screens (static images or simple UI mock)
- FAQ (SEO)
- Email opt-in (optional): “Get weekly macro events + updates”

## SEO Requirements
- Title tag + meta description targeting:
  - “idle finance game”, “investment simulator game”, “compounding game”
- OpenGraph:
  - og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url
- Twitter card
- Sitemap.xml + robots.txt
- Fast LCP; minimal JS on landing

## Copy Constraints
- Do NOT provide investment advice.
- Add disclaimer: “For entertainment only.”
